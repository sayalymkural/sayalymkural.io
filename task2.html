About Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar (born 1987) is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer. Known for his powerful lyrics and social commentary, he is widely considered one of the greatest rappers of his generation.

Key Life Events

  1. 2011 – Released debut album Section.80
  2. 2012 – Breakthrough with good kid, m.A.A.d city
  3. 2015 – Grammy Awards for To Pimp a Butterfly
  4. 2017 – Pulitzer Prize for DAMN.
  5. 2022 – Mr. Morale & The Big Steppers

Contributions

  • Lyrics addressing social justice
  • Influence on modern hip-hop
  • Winner of 17 Grammy Awards
